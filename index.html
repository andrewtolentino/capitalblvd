Why Choose This Location?

The property was just completed in January 2025 and is now ready for leasing. It is located in the heart of Megaworld’s Capital Town development on the main road of Capitol Boulevard, a block away from the biggest McDonald’s restaurant, and just across the Megaworld Bryant Tower and Megaworld Chelsea Tower condominiums. The commercial space consists of two floors with over 100 sqm. on each floor. The stalls on each floor are unfinished, so tenants can easily modify to suit their business needs. The commercial spaces are best suited for a Bank, Coffee shop, Convenient store, Restaurant, Retail store, Medical/Dental offices, and Lawyers Office.
